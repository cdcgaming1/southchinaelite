SOUTH CHINA ELITE

From the editor

編者寄語

W

elcome to the first ever issue of

South China

Elite

. We’re absolutely delighted to have

you as a reader and hope you enjoy SCE for

many years to come.

South China Elite

will showcase fascinating and

extraordinary personalities from across the Greater

Bay area – from Hong Kong to Shenzhen to Guangzhou

to Macau – and elsewhere in Guangdong Province and

South China. We’ll feature people mainly from the

world of business, but we’ll also showcase academics,

entertainers and others making a valuable contribution

in developing the economy in this region which is

amongst the fastest growing in the world.

I had the privilege of being in Beijing this March

during the

liang hui

– the annual plenary sessions

of the National People’s Congress and the Chinese

People’s Political Consultative Conference. It was there

that Chinese Premier Li Keqiang renewed the push for

greater integration and the Guangdong-Hong Kong-

Macau Greater Bay Area concept.

There is no doubt the 21st century will be the

“Asian century”, fuelled in a major way by South China.

Guangdong Province, Hong Kong, Macau and even

the exciting Hengqin project personally supported by

President Xi Jinping have crucial roles to play in this

global shift.

We are indeed privileged to have Senior Fellow of

the Wharton School, Professor Dr Brian To grace the

cover of our maiden issue. Despite holding no less than

six post-graduate degrees fromsuch luminary institutions

as Harvard and Oxford, Professor To is no “theoretical

academic”. As a practice professor he travels the globe

consulting to major Asian governments, militaries and

corporations, coaching CEOs and global leaders and

creating real economic value.

As well as our cover story each issue focusing

on one particular personality making a major

contribution to the region,

South China Elite

will also

have entertaining and informative articles contributed

by some of the most eminent experts in the region, in

our Business, Insights and Lifestyle sections. We hope

these inform your business decisions and in turn grow

your own personal wealth!

Our company, O MEDIA, is no stranger to

publishing. We currently publish

Inside Asian Gaming,

WGM

and

High Life

, which first hit the streets in 2005,

2009 and 2016 respectively – and we have more digital

and print media offerings planned for launch over the

next 18 months or so.

As a wise man once said, Learn from the Elite!

Andrew W Scott

Editor-in-Chief , May 2017

SCE

為讀者們呈獻粵港澳大灣區出類拔萃的人事

物，當中有來自香港、深圳、廣州、澳門的，也有

來自整個廣東省及南華地區各地方的。我們採訪的

對象主要來自商界，但亦有在這全球其中一個經濟

增長最快的地區內，作出寶貴貢獻的學術界人士、

藝人，以及其他業界人士。

本年三月，本人有幸參與在北京舉行的全國人

民代表大會會議和中國人民政治協商會議全國委員

會會議。兩會上，總理李克強表示國家將加強推動

粵港澳大灣區整合發展的概念。

在南華地區的推動下，21世紀將毫無疑問成為

「亞洲的世紀」。廣東省、香港、澳門，甚至主席

習近平親自支持的橫琴項目，都會在全球經濟重心

轉移的過程中，扮演舉足輕重的角色。

SCE

很榮幸邀得賓夕凡尼亞大學禾頓商學院資深研

究員Brian To教授，成為我們的創刊號封面人物。雖然

Brian To教授擁有多於六個由哈佛及牛津等著名大學頒

授的研究生學位，但是他絕非一名「理論派學者」。

作為實踐型教授，他足跡遍布世界各地，為亞洲多國

政府、軍隊及企業提供策略性建議，亦指導多位首席

執行官及全球領導人，從而創造確實的經濟價值。

除了專訪於本地區貢獻良多的封面人物外，

SCE

還邀得區內最卓越的專家，在

SCE

來有助作出商業決定的重要資訊，並從而增加個人

財富！
