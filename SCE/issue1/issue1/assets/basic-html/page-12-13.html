SOUTH CHINA ELITE

Ben Blaschke: First of all Brian, you are very

much a modern globalist in that you consult to

a number of large corporations from a range of

sectors all around the world. Can you provide a bit

more detail on your role?

Dr BrianTo

: As a strategist and a practice professor

for more than 20 years I have the pleasure and privilege

of working on complex situations in different sectors of

industry, government and various leading institutions

around the globe. I work in a number of countries and

am privileged enough to consult and to teach a range

of organizations where I focus largely on issues such

as culture, diversity and the effects of culture and

diversity on productivity.

Working in several countries simultaneously for

these past decades hasmademe evenmore appreciative

and respectful of the importance of community, human

dignity and human performance and the need for

collaboration in all work – from assisting in developing

national competitiveness models to developing global

growth strategies for corporations.

I’m grateful for the opportunity to be involved and

to be able to bring the three key essential key drivers

to all of the projects I work on – namely innovation,

competitiveness and behavioural science.

本思齊：你為全球不同業界的大型企業擔任顧問，可謂

當代全球性策略家。可否解釋一下你扮演的角色？

Brian To博士：作為策略家及實踐型教授，我有幸在過

往20多年間，為全球各地的企業、政府以及頂尖機構處理各

種複雜的問題。我在不同的國家工作，並有幸在多間組織裡

擔任顧問及任教，其中以文化、多元性以及兩者對生產力的

影響等為重點主題。

對於有機會參與並將三大關鍵要素——即創新、競爭

力及行為科學——應用到我負責的項目，我感到十分榮幸。
