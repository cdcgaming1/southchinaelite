How would you characterize the

Chinese economy?

As it is unfolding, clearly the economy is in a major

transition. With double digit growth for decades, the

economy is moving from a hyper emerging economy to

strategic positioning in the global economic landscapewith

a comfortable GDP somewhere within the 6% to 8% range.

Further, in order to sustain global growth, the

Central Government saw a compelling necessity to

engage in a disciplined effort to align the leadership

and management of both SOE’s and corporate China

through principled leadership and ethical management

practices.

Understandably, this has caused the highlighting

of gaps in management processes, facilitation methods

and leadership practices. Today, the consequences

of significant corruption are challenging where past

practices can no longer be the modus operandi for

leaders in both facilitating business growth and

expansion. The new generation has to adopt principled

and ethical leadership practices. And it has to be deeply

rooted in one’s consciousness because under the present

leadership of China, this is obviously not an invitation

but more of a demand.

你認為中國經濟有何特徵？

中國經濟顯然處於關鍵的轉型階段。經歷了數十年的每

年雙位數增長後，中國經濟從新興經濟體系高速增長階段，

至今在全球經濟版圖中立下一個策略性定位，而國內生產總

值則維持在每年6%至8%的健康水平。

與此同時，為了維持全球經濟增長，中央政府明白必須

通過合乎道德倫理的領導管理方法及有紀律的方式，整治國

有企業與中國企業的管理措施。

這樣一來，中國在管理過程、促進發展和領導管理方式

的弊端，便開始為人們所注視。現在，我們明白到貪污問題

的嚴重後果，領導者已不能再以舊有作風和慣常做法去推動

經濟發展。新一代領導者須採取合乎道德倫理的領導管理方

式。這需要由心而發地進行，因為在中國當前的領導管理生

態中，革新顯然不是選擇，而是必須的。

On China’s economy and its future

論中國經濟及其前景

What can you say about China and its

environment?

There are possibly billions of opinions on how

China is doing with the economy and its environmental

agenda, from human rights to labour unrest. It has

been the whipping boy of foreign leaders in their

campaign platforms this year but despite perpetual

negative assaults, China’s economy has still performed

well compared to other leading nations who’s GDP has

struggled to reach even 3%. And as chill winds blow

across all of Asia, these challenges for China are not

trivial but mammoth in scope and consequence.

Whilst many who travel to Beijing and Shanghai

realize and have become conditioned to the perpetual

smog, pollution and dark skies above, a government

urbanization plan unfolded earlier this year and this

strategy aims to ultimately increase the residential and

commercial density, reducing commuting distance and

accommodating the endless millions of people moving

to the cities each year.

It’s a race against time in order to reduce pollution

and prepare to accommodate an expected 100 million-plus

peoplemoving into the country’s cities by 2020. The greatest

migration on planet earthwill undoubtedly continue.

你如何看待中國及其環境問題？

針對中國經濟、環境、人權及勞工紛爭等議題而發表的

意見，數目猶如恒河沙數。不少國家的政府正值大選年，無

不就上述議題對中國大肆抨擊。儘管批評聲音不斷，但是與

其它先進國家勉強取得的3%經濟增長率相比，中國經濟依然

表現良好。寒風席捲亞洲各地時，這些挑戰對中國絕非無足

輕重，而是會帶來引致嚴重後果的深廣影響。

當不少前往北京及上海的旅客，對城市上空連年不斷的

霧霾及污染習以為常之時，政府就於本年初推出新的城市化

計劃，力圖提高住宅及商業密度、減低兩者距離，並為每年

數以百萬從鄉村移居城市的人口提供安身之所。

減低污染、為2020年前從鄉村移居城市的1億人口提供

安身之所，已刻不容緩。這地球上規模最大的移民潮，無疑

會繼續下去。
