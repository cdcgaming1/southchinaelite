“We have something for everyone” Ewa explains

with visible excitement. “We cover all the different

market segments, from speedboats to luxurious, tailor-

made yachts. Beneteau, one of the brands we represent,

has motorboats that would be perfect for a beginner or

someone who doesn’t want to spend too much money.

On the other end of the spectrum is Sanlorenzo, a brand

known for its super yachts.”

Indeed, Simpson Marine’s portfolio includes an

impressive list of eight brands represented in China,

Hong Kong, Indonesia, Malaysia, Singapore, Taiwan and

Thailand. They include Montecarlo Yachts, Montecarlo,

Viking, CNB, Beneteau Sailing Yachts and family friendly

brand Lagoon Catamarans.

Ewa 興奮之情溢於言表，「我們能滿足各種需求，涵蓋

所有不同的細分市場，從快艇到豪華訂製遊艇。博納多是我

們旗下的代理品牌之一，他們的摩托艇對初學者或不想太過

破費的顧客來說，是理想之選。而聖勞倫佐則是一個以超級

遊艇著稱的品牌。」

辛普森遊艇的產品目錄共有八個品牌，在中國、香港、

印度尼西亞、馬來西亞、新加坡、台灣和泰國市場推出，其

中包括蒙地卡羅遊艇、蒙地卡羅、Viking、CNB、博納多帆

船，以及適合家庭的品牌藍高。
