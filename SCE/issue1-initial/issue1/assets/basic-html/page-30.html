SOUTH CHINA ELITE

The additional compliance costs, plus the

requirements to strengthen internal IT capabilities to

capture any undesirable activities of clients made the

private banking and wealth management business for

the smaller banks and units unprofitable. “Red flags”

and improved KYC (Know Your Customer) profiles

added tremendous costs to the banks. Many banks

found it futile to continuously appease the insatiable

regulators.

The prognosis by the author in his book

Private

Banking & Wealth Management in Asia – An Insider

Perspective

(2014, Warrior Books) that there would

be only two categories of private banking and wealth

management banks which would survive the current

and future environment is unfolding. The private

banking and wealth management businesses of Credit

Agricole, ING, Societe Generale, Barclays, Coutts and

ABN AMRO have been sold. A few more in the middle-

tier will become antiquity in the near future.

額外的法規審查成本，以及強化監察客戶不當行為的

內部科技所引致的支出，均令小型銀行以及部機構的私人銀

行與財富管理業務變得無利可圖。風險警示及「認識你的客

戶」資料系統，令銀行的成本激增，以至不少銀行認為，不

斷滿足監管機構日新月異的要求，實在徒勞無功。

筆者在《亞洲私人銀行與財富管理——內行人觀點》

（

Private Banking & Wealth Management in Asia – An

Insider Perspective

）（2014年， Warrior Books出版）一

書中，預期只有兩類私人銀行與財富管理機構能在當前以及

未來的環境下生存。這預測正逐漸實現——法國農業信貸銀

行、ING集團、興業銀行、巴克萊銀行、Coutts銀行以及荷蘭

銀行的私人銀行與財富管理業務，均已被出售，而另外數個

中型銀行的相關部門，亦快將成為歷史。

wealthmanagement banks that can ride out the current

storm are the mega global banks such as UBS, Credit

Suisse, Citibank and JP Morgan; and the boutique or

“pure-play” banks such as Pictet, LGT, EFG and Union

Bancaire Privee.

This scenario will prevail until the pendulum of

regulatory bearings moves from extreme tightness

to the pivotal center. But alas, the new CRS (Common

Reporting System) signed by Hong Kong, Singapore,

China and most Southeast Asian counties will come into

effect in 2018. This would cast the industry into another

impasse, where all the banks and financial institutions

are required to file client-sensitive data to the CRS. This

database would be shared internationally to prevent

money laundering activities and any undesirable

movements of funds. The final ramifications are still

unknown.

Is it worthwhile, then, to remain in the private

banking and wealth management business or call it a

day? Does the industry still have a future?

最後，兩類能安然渡過當前風暴的私人銀行與財富管

理機構，將是全球性大型銀行如瑞士銀行、瑞士信貸、花

旗銀行和摩根大通等，以及如Pictet、LGT、EFG及Union

Bancaire Privée等小型精品或「單一業務」機構。

在監管的發展趨勢從矯枉過正回歸至正常力度前，此情

況將會持續。然而，香港、新加坡、中國以及大部分東南亞國

家簽署的全新共同申報準則（Common Reporting System），將

於2018年生效。根據系統要求，所有銀行及金融機構均須呈交

客戶的敏感資料。這將令業界陷入另一個僵局。此數據庫是國

際共享的，以防止洗黑錢活動及其它不當資金流動。其最終結

果為何，現在還不得而知。

那麼，我們是否還值得繼續私人銀行和財富管理的業

務？還是應該打退堂鼓？這個行業是否還有未來？
