Most corporations neglect the missing piece in

the middle. When formulating strategy, there is an

impatience in executing that strategy without first

developing it. When you jump from formulation

to execution you neglect the key fact which is that it

should go from formulation to development – then

execution. Development is an important detail because

this is where innovation takes place. Companies that

try to jump this stage don’t benefit from innovative

processes. Too often it is a rehash of what has been

done before so they don’t benefit from the presence

of new ideas, new initiatives and thinking differently

about markets.

This has to be appreciated because research into

product cycles shows them to be increasingly rapid.

Previously, product cycles would run two or three

years. Now they run two or three months. So engaging

themarket with themand getting feedback is necessary.

The research suggests that of all the new products

going into the market each year, more than 68% of

them will be taken off the market within 18 months.

如企業忽略了發展策略這階段，則無法享受創新過程中獲

這是極為重要的，因為研究顯示，產品週期正變得愈
