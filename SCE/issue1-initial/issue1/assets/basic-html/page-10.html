SOUTH CHINA ELITE

Having long been an avid sports fan, Ben put his

passion to good use – embarking on a decade-long

career as a sports journalist for leading Australian

newspapers and sports magazines before joining

O MEDIA in Macau in 2013. Aside from covering

some of the world’s biggest sporting events,

including the 2008 Olympic Games in Beijing, Ben

has long had an interest in big industry which has

led him to cover the gaming sector and beyond in

Macau and Hong Kong. In his spare time Ben loves

good food, fine wine and a touch of pampering!
