多數企業管理者均意識到經濟週期，希望以低

廉的價格購買資產，並在低谷過後不久以較低

的價格擴展業務。與此同時，他們均希望在經

濟週期的高峰時出售其資產、業務或股票。可惜，他們總是

因錯失最佳時機而感到遺憾。股市崩潰時，他們知道經濟衰

退已無可扭轉；資產價格上升時，他們卻認為擴展業務成本

高昂。

本文旨在討論如何以更佳的企業策略應對經濟週期，亦

列出有助企業管理者乃至專業經濟師，預測未來高峰和低谷

的各項指標。

M

ost business executives are aware of riding an

economic cycle. They wish to buy assets at low

prices and expand their business at cheaper

costs shortly after a trough. Meanwhile, they all wish

to sell their assets, their business and their shares at the

peak of a cycle. Unfortunately these business executives

are always disappointed at missing the best timings.

When the stock market crashes, they realize recession

is already there. When asset prices rise, they find it costly

to expand their business.

This article aims to discuss some better corporate

strategies to deal with economic cycles. It also identifies

indicators that help business executives to mirror

professional economists in predicting upcoming peaks

and troughs.
