SOUTH CHINA ELITE

我們能促進領導管理及勇氣嗎？

所有首席執行官、執行董事及經理等，均直覺地、本能

地知道，無論企業的規模及風格為何，都有責任和義務推動

企業內的領導管理及勇氣。在這個競爭異常激烈的變革時期

出現的挑戰，可能是我們沒有準備好面對的，又或是未有接

受過訓練去應對的。領導管理需要過人的勇氣、人際溝通技

巧，以及對員工表現的熱誠、關注與包容。

儘管存在挑戰，有時候它們會與個人偏好和情況背道而

馳。然而，具備前瞻性並激勵他人更積極參與，仍然是我們

的主要機會之一。通過了解他人和自己，我們就能以合乎道

德倫理且高效的方式，為競爭做好準備。

做人行事要勇敢，還要謹記——待人以禮，總比咄咄逼

人為妙。

Can we advance leadership and

courage?

All CEOs, Managing Directors and Managers

intuitively and instinctively know that we have a duty

and responsibility to advocate leadership and courage

throughout the organization regardless of size and

shape. In this period of hypercompetitive change, many

challenges are presented that we may not be particularly

prepared for or were not trained for. Leadership requires

courage and superior interpersonal skills as well as a

passion for human performance and a focus on inclusion.

At times this may seem at odds with individual

preference and circumstances, albeit sometimes

challenging. But having a vision of what should be

done and inspiring others to greater involvement and

participation remains one of our greatest opportunities.

Clearly by understanding others and ourselves better, we

can best prepare for competition and change ethically

and productively.

Be courageous and remember, it’s better to always

be kind than always be right.
