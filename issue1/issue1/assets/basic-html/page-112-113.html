SOUTH CHINA ELITE

“We’re definitely trying to push it higher,” he says.

“I mean, we’ve got a lot of successful females, women

who are very strong and have very strong careers. At

the same time our cars have become more accessible.

“Our newest cars like we have now, the V10 and V12

derivative, both have semi-automatic gear boxes – they

are not manual – and there are different modes that we

can set to change the dynamics of the car. Both cars have

three modes – the Strada (street), Sport and Corsa (race

course) – so when you put it into Strada you essentially

have a fully automatic car where the damper is softened,

the steering becomes light, it’s easier to park and the

engine is softer so you can listen to music.

“We are also doing lots of test drives now so even

when a male comes in to look at the car and they are going

to do a test drive, I always ask them to bring their wife ...

because the wife is the decision maker of the household!

I ask them to take a drive and once they get into the car

it’s actually quite easy. The visibility is great and also, in a

Lamborghini you definitely get respect on the road. When

you change lanes, people let you pass. They want to look

at the car and they also don’t want to scratch you up! So

they are great on the road and yes, we have seen lots of

women coming into the Lamborghini family.”

他說︰「我們正著力推動這個市場。如今有不少成功

女性，而我們的車型亦較以往更加容易操作。像我們最新

車型V10和V12的兩條動力線都是非手動的，而是備有半自

動變速箱；另外，我們還有其它可調節的模式，可供我們

改變車的動力。兩條動力線都提供Strada公路、Sport運動

和Corsa賽道三種駕駛模式。當你調到Strada模式，基本就

是一輛全自動車，減震變軟，方向盤變輕，易泊車，同時

引擎聲變小，可以聽音樂。」

「我們還做過很多試駕。男士來看車並且想試駕時，

我總會請他們帶上太太，因為太太是掌握大權的人！我會

請他們試駕，一旦進了車裡，事情就變得容易了。品牌辨

識度很高，在路上必定讓你備受禮遇——變道時，其它車

都會禮讓；他們想端詳一番，當然也不想刮擦到你的車！

駕駛林寶基尼上路絕對是很棒的體驗。我們確實看到越來

越多女性加入到林寶基尼大家庭。」
