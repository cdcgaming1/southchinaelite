SOUTH CHINA ELITE

BB: You’re a change agent then?

BT:

In every situation and instance you’re a

facilitator of change as an advisor or professor. Yes,

I believe all of us are fundamentally change agents,

albeit often we are reluctant to change ourselves.

BB: Finally Brian, what led you in this career

direction?

BT:

It’s merely the fulfilment of a childhood

dream. I used to listen to my father and the meetings

that he had. I was only four or five years of age and I

always wanted to be able to contribute to business and

always wanted to be a business consultant. But I was

ambitious as a child. I always wanted to be a professor

too. I wanted to pass knowledge onto others. And I also

wanted to be in the military! I had aspirations for all

of those things. Today I am involved with the military,

with non-profits, I’m heavily involved in academia as

well as advising CEOs and board members.

本思齊：你是帶動變革者嗎？

Brian To博士：身為顧問或教授的人，在任何情況下均是

協助變革的因素。我認為我們本質上都是促進變革的因素，儘

管我們往往不願意改變自己。

本思齊：最後，驅使你選擇這個事業的原因為何？

Brian To博士：我只是在實現自己的童年夢想。小時候，

我常聽家父說他參與過的會議。那時候，我年僅四五歲，卻一

直想為企業作貢獻，並成為商業顧問。我也雄心勃勃要成為教

授，將知識傳給別人。同時，我又想加入軍隊！小時候，我懷

有多個理想。今天，我如願以償地參與軍事、非牟利組織及學

術界等項目，並為首席執行官和董事局成員擔任顧問。
