SOUTH CHINA ELITE

Over the next 10 years, Frank Knight projected

that a surge of 71% of billionaires in Asia would attain

almost the same number as in the US. The billionaires

in China and India are projected to increase by 75%

and 105% respectively over the next decade.

As individual wealth continues to multiply at such

an awesome scale, the need for private banking and

wealth management business is indisputable.

The critical success factors are to develop

strategies and modus operandi that are befitting to

the banks’ own strengths and weaknesses, are cost

effective and sustainable. The good old days of doing

private banking and wealth management business has

gone with the wind.

The rapidly changing environment and market

place demand that banks and their senior management

adapt to the new paradigms. Any banks and financial

institutions still clinging to their old-fashioned ways

will become relics.
