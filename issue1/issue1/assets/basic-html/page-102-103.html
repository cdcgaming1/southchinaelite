SOUTH CHINA ELITE

SANLORENZO 460

“That is why some of our clients enjoy having their

yachts in countries such as Thailand. They know and feel

secure in the region and Thailand has a good reputation

for being safe and beautiful,” she explains.

The process might seem somewhat daunting for a

beginner, but Ewa assures us it doesn’t have to be.

“You really need to allow people to experience it

for themselves,” she continues. “In Europe for example,

we are used to seeing marinas and boats, be they big or

small, but in Asia it’s still a relatively new reality.”

Yachting is about more than just a boat, it’s

a lifestyle. Feeling the sunshine on your skin and

experiencing the freedom of the sea are just some of

the joys of owning a boat. And reading through all of

the options Simpson Marine has on offer is certainly

enticing – elegant interiors with beautiful finishes,

enjoying utmost comfort as you rip through the waves.

It is the ultimate personal adventure.

「正因如此，我們的一些客戶選擇在泰國等地擁有遊

艇。他們知道、感到泰國較安全，並有安全、美麗出海勝地

的良好聲譽。」

這個過程對初學者來說，似乎有點令人望而生畏，但

Ewa向我們保證，事情並非一定如此。

她繼續說︰「人們需要親自去體驗。例如在歐洲，我們

習慣看到大大小小的碼頭和遊艇。但在亞洲，它仍是較新的

現象。」

遊艇的意義絕不僅限於船隻本身，它代表一種生活方

式。讓肌膚與陽光親密接觸以及體驗海洋的自由，只是其中

的一些樂趣。翻閱辛普森遊艇的產品目錄，著實讓人怦然心

動——優雅的內飾與悅目的外觀，在乘風破浪時讓您享受最

佳的舒適。這是個人冒險的終極體驗。
