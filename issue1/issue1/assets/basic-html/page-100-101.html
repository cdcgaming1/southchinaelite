SOUTH CHINA ELITE

Services are extensive with the company not limiting

itself to just selling boats. They also provide assistance

once the purchase is completed as well as ensuring one

of their experts personally goes to the shipping yards to

ensure maximum quality and client satisfaction.

Ewa explains that China remains a relatively new

market but perceptions are changing fast.

“We’ve noticed a great difference in five years,

the number of private owners is increasing,” she says.

“We think the first step for this to happen is investing

in Charter more. This way individuals can experience

first-hand the joy of being close to the sea.”

She adds that the high import tax in China, more

than 40%, remains a barrier to the business.

公司服務範圍廣泛，除出售遊艇之外，亦提供廣泛的售

後服務，並會派送一位專家親自前往貨運場地，以確保最高

的品質和客戶滿意度。

Ewa解釋說，中國仍是一個相對較新的市場，但人們對

遊艇的認識正迅速轉變。

「我們留意到過去五年裡的巨大變化，私人擁有者的數

量持續增加。我們認為，第一步是在租賃業務上增加投資，

以令更多人可親身體驗親近大海的樂趣。」

她補充說，中國進口稅高達40%以上，仍是這項產業的

一個絆腳石。
