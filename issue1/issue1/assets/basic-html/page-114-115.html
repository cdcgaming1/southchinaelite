SOUTH CHINA ELITE

Which brings us to one of Lamborghini’s main

selling points – apart, of course, from the sheer beauty

of the cars themselves.

“Our customers can create the car that they want,”

Wong says. “Thanks to our personalization program, we

can basically do anything they can think of and that really

broadens our customer base.

“We had one guy with an iPad cover in matte purple

and he loved the shade so much that we sent the cover

to the factory so they could personalize the paint to the

same color for his Huracán.

“There was another lady who wanted her calipers

in leopard skin.

“We can even do the same for the interiors, such

as how we stitch the leather of the seats. We had one

customer with his name stitched into one side and the

girlfriend’s name on the other side. The factory came

back and said, ‘What happens if they break up?’ but that

was okay. I told them, ‘He can just give her the car and

come back to buy a new one for himself!’”

除了車本身的純粹魅力，林寶基尼其實還有別的主要

賣點。

黃卓徽表示，「顧客可隨心所欲打造自己的座駕。我

們的私人訂製服務，基本上可以滿足任何要求。這絕對拓

寬了我們的客戶群。」

「有一位客戶非常喜歡自己iPad外殼的顏色，啞光

紫。我們將外殼寄至工廠，為他的Huracán定制個性化油

漆。另外還有一位女士想將卡鉗塗成豹紋。」

「我們甚至可以有訂製內飾，比如怎樣縫製皮革座

椅。有一位客戶想把他的名字縫在一邊，女友的名字縫在

另一邊。工廠回來問我，如果兩人分手了怎麼辦？不過這

也不是問題，我告訴他們，他可以把這台車送給她，然後

為自己買台新車！」
