SOUTH CHINA ELITE

“He was sailing around the continent with his

girlfriend and decided to build his own boat,” explains

Marketing Manager Ewa Stachurska. “To his surprise,

as soon as he reached Taiwan he was approached by a

buyer, wishing to purchase it.”

The story repeated itself twice before he realized

he should turn his passion into a business – and that’s

how the yacht sales and charter company was founded,

focusing on meeting the needs of the developing marine

industry in Asia. By the end of the 1980s, the company

had been appointed as regional distributor for several

leading European and Asian yacht brands. Hong Kong

was first choice because the market was far more mature

than in other destinations around the region. Today, the

company has more than 20 offices in seven countries.

集團市場經理 Ewa Stachurska 解釋說︰「麥克和女友

環球航行期間，決定打造自己夢想中的遊艇。意外的是，抵

達台灣之後，立即有買家找他，希望購買他的遊艇。」

同樣的事發生兩次之後，他意識到可以將熱情與商機結

合起來。於是，一家遊艇銷售和租賃公司便應運而生，旨在

滿足亞洲市場的需求。到了1980年代後期，公司已經成為多

個歐洲及亞洲領先遊艇品牌的區域經銷商。香港是首選，因

為香港的市場比區內其它地方要成熟很多。如今，公司在七

個國家設有二十多個辦事處。
